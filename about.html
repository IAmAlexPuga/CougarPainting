About Our Team

Meet our dedicated full-time experts, each bringing unique specialties to our dynamic team. While we collaborate with talented contractors, this page highlights the core members driving our ongoing success.

Owner | Juan

Meet Juan, a seasoned professional with over 25 years of experience in the art of remodeling houses. With a passion for innovation and a love for trying new things, Juan brings fresh perspectives and creative solutions to every project. When not busy transforming homes, he enjoys winding down with weekly movie nights, favoring the adrenaline rush of action films. Juan has been at the helm of our company for the past 10 years, leading our team to excellence in the world of home improvement. Off-duty, you can often find him exploring new culinary horizons, relishing the delight of trying new and exotic foods.

Software Engineer & Project Manager | Alex

Meet Alex, a versatile member of our team. Alex holds a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering from WSU and currently serves as our software engineer and project manager. With a strong background in technology and a passion for innovative solutions, he plays a pivotal role in steering our projects toward success. When not immersed in the world of software, Alex enjoys his downtime, tending to his Traeger grill to create delectable smoked dishes. Alex's multifaceted interests, from technology to culinary delights and his affection for animals, make him a valuable and well-rounded team member.

Professional Painter & Landscaper | Roman

Meet Roman, a dedicated member of our team. With six years of experience as a professional landscaper and painter, Roman's passion for transforming outdoor and indoor spaces shines through his work. Although he excels in both, Roman's true love is painting, where he brings artistic flair and meticulous attention to detail. Beyond his career, Roman aspires to fulfill his dream job of becoming a coach, inspiring and mentoring others to achieve their goals. Recently stepping into the world of fatherhood, he is as committed to his family as he is to his craft, embodying the qualities of hard work and devotion in all aspects of life.
